Why Choose Code IT?

  • Practical Oriented Training: We focus on hands-on learning with real-world projects.

  • Affordable Fees: We provide quality education at competitive prices.
  • Job Placement Support: We assist our students in finding suitable employment opportunities.

2. Mobile App Development

3. UI/UX Design

Master the principles of user interface and user experience design with industry-standard tools like Figma and Adobe XD.

4. Digital Marketing

Our Training Methodology

At Code IT, we believe in learning by doing. Our training methodology includes:

  • Regular assignments and projects
  • One-on-one doubt clearing sessions
  • Mock interviews and resume building workshops
  • Internship opportunities for deserving students
